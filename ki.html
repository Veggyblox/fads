Real Madrid Club de Fútbol , commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a football club based in Madrid, Spain. The club compete in La Liga, the top tier of Spanish football. Founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club,the club have traditionally worn a white home kit. The honorific title real is Spanish for "royal" and was given by King Alfonso XIIIin 1920, as well as the crown being added
